人生一路 (Life is a journey)

一度決めたら 二度とは変えぬ
これが自分の 生きる道
泣くな迷うな 苦しみ抜いて
人は望みを はたすのさ

Once I decide I will never change again
This is my way of living
Don't cry, don't hesitate, get rid of the pain
People achieve their wishes

 
雪の深さに 埋もれて耐えて
麦は芽を出す 春を待つ
生きる試練に 身をさらすとも
意地をつらぬく 人になれ

Buried in the depth of the snow and bearing
Wheat waits for spring to sprout
Friend who exposes to life tests
Be someone who carries out his pride


 
胸に根性の 炎を抱いて
決めたこの道 まっしぐら
明日にかけよう 人生一路
花は苦労の 風に咲け

Embrace in your chest the flames of determination
Go full speed by the road you chose
Bet on tomorrow, life is a journey
Flowers will bloom in the wind of hardship
